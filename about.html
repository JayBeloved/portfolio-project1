A portfolio is a collection of work samples that you can bring to an interview, send to a prospective employer, or even post online. There are basically three kinds of portfolios which are:

  1. A Showcase Portfolio
  2. An Assessment Portfolio
  3. A Development Portfolio

A portfolio site is an extension of a freelancer's (or company's) résumé. The ultimate purpose of a portfolio website is to provide a way for you to land more clients, whether that means freelance work, more clients for your agency or employment at a company.
